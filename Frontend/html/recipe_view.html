Recipe Name

Ingredients

1 pound lean ground beef ½ cup bread crumbs 3 tablespoons minced onion 2 tablespoons water 1 large egg 1 (8 ounce) can jellied cranberry sauce ¾ cup chili sauce 1 tablespoon brown sugar 1 ½ teaspoons lemon juice

recipe method description

Directions Gather the ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Mix ground beef, bread crumbs, onion, water, and egg together in a large bowl. Form into small meatballs and arrange on a nonstick baking sheet. Bake in the preheated oven for 20 to 25 minutes, turning once. Stir cranberry sauce, chili sauce, brown sugar, and lemon juice together in a large saucepan over low heat until smooth.
